OTT · Platform Strategy

YouTube Unified Platform Strategy

One app. Two experiences. A behavioural engagement framework + unified audio-video platform built to solve creator monetisation at scale.

Explore strategy →
01
Audio Mode
A native background-play mode for music, podcasts, and long-form content. Seamlessly switch between Video and Audio experience inside the same app — keeping users inside YouTube's ecosystem even with screen off.
Retention
02
Active Audience Framework
Subscribers are classified into Active, Semi-Active, and Inactive segments based on watch duration, frequency, and interaction — eliminating vanity metrics and giving creators real audience visibility. Inactive users (90+ days) receive automated re-engagement prompts.
Algorithm
03
Creator Fair Pay System
Revenue tied to engagement depth, not just ad impressions. Fairer metrics mean realistic credibility for creators, higher CPM for advertisers, and a healthier creator growth environment — especially for sub-100K channels.
Monetisation
04
Lyric Timestamps + Vibe Reactions
Music-first conversation features inside Audio Mode: share specific lyric timestamps as emotional signals, react to content with soundbite reactions. Music as language, not just background noise.

Music · Social Product

Tuning — Speak in Lyrics. Share the Vibe.

A music-first communication layer built on Spotify. Express emotions through song snippets, lyric timestamps, and vibe reactions.

Explore app flow →
01
Spotify Home → Tap to open Tuning
Tuning lives inside Spotify as a communication layer. Users tap the Tuning icon from the home screen to access their music-based conversations — no separate app, zero friction entry.
Screen 1
02
Conversations — Music as Messages
Your Tuning inbox shows conversations — each thread anchored by the last song shared. Riya sent "Raataan Lambiyan." Karthik sent "Tera Yaar Hoon Main." Music replaces text as the primary communication signal.
Screen 2
03
Chat — React, Send a Song, Search
Inside a chat: share songs with lyric timestamps, react with vibe reactions (not emojis — musical reactions), or search any track to send. "No keyboard. Just music." — the core Tuning principle.
Screen 3
04
Now Playing → Chat via Tuning
From any Now Playing screen, tap "Chat via Tuning" in the options menu to instantly share that track with a friend. The song carries emotional context — no caption needed.
Screen 4 & 5
05
Send To — Share with One Tap
A contact picker shows friends, their last-shared song, and time — select and send. Frictionless music sharing that feels as natural as sending a WhatsApp message.
