YouTube Unified Platform

OTT · Platform Strategy

One app. Two experiences. A behavioural engagement framework + audio-video platform built to solve creator monetisation at scale.

Explore strategy →
01
Audio Mode
Native background-play for music, podcasts, and long-form content. Switch between Video and Audio inside the same app — keeping users inside YouTube's ecosystem even with screen off.
Retention
02
Active Audience Framework
Subscribers classified as Active, Semi-Active, or Inactive based on watch duration, frequency, and interaction — eliminating vanity metrics and giving creators real audience visibility. 90-day inactivity triggers automated re-engagement.
Algorithm
03
Creator Fair Pay System
Revenue tied to engagement depth, not just ad impressions. Fairer metrics → realistic credibility for creators, higher CPM for advertisers, healthier creator ecosystem especially at sub-100K scale.
Monetisation
04
Lyric Timestamps + Vibe Reactions
Music-first social features inside Audio Mode: share specific lyric timestamps as emotional signals, react with soundbite reactions. Music as language — not just background noise.
